## Random Packed Flag Trigger
When entering the trigger, the level will pick out a random flag out of the packed flags by Packed Flags Controller, when this flag is picked out and set, the flag will not be picked out again until all other flags in the bag are all picked out, then the bag will reset
So, imagine that you put a bunch of sticks in a pot, you pick out a stick and throw it away, but if you throw them all away, then you start picking them up and put them back into the pot
